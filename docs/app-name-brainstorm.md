# App Name Brainstorm

Current name: **picsort**

## Candidates

### Photography-native
- **Cull** — "culling" is the exact word photographers use for sorting through a shoot. One syllable, zero ambiguity. Very on-brand.
- **Culla** — softer version if `Cull` feels too blunt

### Action / swipe energy
- **Flic** — sounds like "flick" (the gesture), short, cool
- **Glide** — smooth, implies effortless sorting
- **Sweep** — bonus: already matches the "Duplicate Sweep" feature name inside the app

### Camera roll imagery
- **Rollcut** — camera roll + cutting the bad ones. Clear meaning, no existing major app
- **Slidebox** — nostalgic (slide film boxes), evocative of organizing physical photos

## Ranking

1. **Cull** — best fit for the concept, punchy, searchable, no fluff
2. **Flic** — most fun to say, gesture-native
3. **Sweep** — cohesive with the duplicate feature, clean verb

## Cull vs Culla

| | Cull | Culla |
|---|---|---|
| Syllables | 1 | 2 |
| Feel | Sharp, photographer-native | Soft, approachable |
| Risk | "cull" has negative connotations outside photography | None |
| Brand pattern | Verb-as-name | Proper noun (Canva, Figma, Halide) |
| Domain | Likely taken or expensive | More likely available |

**Verdict: Culla is the stronger product name.** "Cull" is the concept, Culla is the brand.

## Logo Prompts for Recraft.ai

Selected name: **Cull**. App accent color: teal `#3AAFA9`. Target: 1024×1024 iOS app icon.

### Direction 1 — Card stack with gesture
> Minimalist iOS app icon. A clean stack of square photo cards, top card slightly rotated left as if being swiped away. Flat vector style. Teal and white on a deep charcoal background. No gradients, no shadows. Geometric, precise.

### Direction 2 — Lettermark "C" as a lens
> App icon logo. The letter C formed by a partial camera lens ring, open on the right side suggesting selection or cutting. Flat vector, single weight stroke. Teal (#3AAFA9) on white. Minimal, modern, Swiss design influence.

### Direction 3 — Photo frame + scissors concept
> Minimal app icon. A simple photo frame icon with a clean diagonal cut through it, representing selection and culling. Flat 2D vector. White icon on solid teal background. No texture, no gradients. iOS-style icon shape.

### Direction 4 — Abstracted viewfinder crosshair
> iOS app icon. Minimalist viewfinder crosshair inside a rounded square, with one quadrant filled in teal suggesting a deliberate selection. Flat vector illustration. Monochromatic teal palette, white background. Geometric and precise.

### Direction 5 — Single photo with a bold checkmark
> App icon. Clean flat illustration of a photo thumbnail with a large bold checkmark overlaid, the rest slightly faded — conveying "the one kept from many." Teal accent on off-white. Minimal, no drop shadows, vector art.

### Recraft tips
- Set style to **"Vector illustration"** or **"Icon"** — not photo-realistic
- Lock aspect ratio to **1:1**, export at **1024×1024**
- Try each prompt with both dark and light background variants
- Use Recraft's **"Vectorize"** feature after generating to get a clean SVG for Xcode
